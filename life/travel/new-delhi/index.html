About #

New Delhi is an ur­ban dis­trict of Delhi which serves as the cap­i­tal of India and seat of all three branches of the Government of India. The foun­da­tion stone of the city was laid by Emperor George V dur­ing the Delhi Durbar of 1911. Text from Wikipedia

I was born and raised in New Delhi and spent most of my life there, un­til I moved to Enschede in 2017. Since then, I visit New Delhi about 3 or 4 times every year.
