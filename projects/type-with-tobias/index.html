From September to December 2019, I stud­ied Typography at Santa Clara University in California as part of my mi­nor se­mes­ter abroad. One of the pro­jects I de­signed was a brochure, ticket, and de­sign sys­tem for a ty­pog­ra­phy event fea­tur­ing iconic de­signer Tobias Frere-Jones.

Because there were very few high-res­o­lu­tion pho­tos of Tobias, I used this vec­tor­ized im­age style with bright col­ors.
