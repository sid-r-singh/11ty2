In April 2018, I made the ﬁrst com­mit to Uppload (56bd930) and pitched it as an idea to Michael, the founder of El Niño, to fund the de­vel­op­ment of an open-source JavaScript ﬁle up­loader, some­thing that the web des­per­ately needed. Later that sum­mer, we launched Uppload and started us­ing it in our pro­jects.

Today, I’m proud to launch Uppload v2, a rewrite from the group up in TypeScript, with sup­port for over 30 a-la-carte plu­g­ins, keep­ing the same pow­er­ful API, easy-to-use in­ter­face, and browser sup­port.
